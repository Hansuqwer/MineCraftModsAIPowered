🎯 OBJECTIVE: Test Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech functionality

📱 ACCESS:
  1. Deploy APK to Android device: adb install build/app/outputs/flutter-apk/app-release.apk
  2. Open app
  3. Navigate to: /voice-test screen
  4. See two status cards at top (should both show ✅)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

🧪 TEST 1: Speech-to-Text (T1.1)
  Button: "Test Speech Input"
  Action: Click → Listen → Speak clearly when it says "Listening..."
  Example: "I want a red dragon"
  Success: See text appear in "Recognized Text" box
  Expect: 🎤 VOICE TEST: ✅ Speech recognized: [your words]

🔊 TEST 2: Text-to-Speech Hello (T1.2)
  Button: "Test TTS: Hello"
  Action: Click → Listen for audio
  Expected: Hear "Hello! This is Crafta speaking!"
  Success: Clear voice output, no errors

🎉 TEST 3: Text-to-Speech Celebration (T1.3)
  Button: "Test TTS: Celebration"
  Action: Click → Listen for audio
  Expected: Hear "Great job! You are amazing!"
  Success: Different tone from Test 2

🔄 TEST 4: Complete Voice Loop (T1.4) ⭐ MOST IMPORTANT
  Button: "Test Complete Voice Loop"
  Action: Click → Listen for greeting → Speak when prompted → Hear echo
  Steps:
    1. Hear: "Hello! I am Crafta. Please say something!"
    2. Say: "I want a blue sword" or "Make me a flying horse"
    3. Wait (15 seconds listening)
    4. Hear: "You said: [your words]"
  Success: Full conversation works end-to-end

⚠️  IMPORTANT:

  • Check device is NOT MUTED
